Welcome from Sarah and Katherine...

We believe that learning needs to be active, purposeful and to include lots of practice questions.

The reformed A levels and GCSEs are considerably more challenging than the previous qualifications with a much greater emphasis on the application of knowledge. We aim, therefore, to deliver tuition sessions that first ensure that the basic concepts are understood and then gradually build up the complexity with the aid of lots of exam question practice. In our experience, difficulties in Mathematics and Science arise because a key point has not been understood, possibly resulting from confusing delivery in large and busy classrooms, and then because of time pressures the misunderstanding has been compounded as further topics have been taught. Just because something has been taught by the teacher, does not mean that it has been learned by the student.

We are looking forward to helping students to learn and achieve their potential in future exams.

Services offered:

Individual tuition
Small group tuition
Assessment marking and feedback
Study skills support
UCAS support

Please contact us to discuss the level of service that you require and the price. All the sessions will be delivered online using Google Meet and an interactive whiteboard.
